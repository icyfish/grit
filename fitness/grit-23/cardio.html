# 01. WARM-UP

  • Jog OTS 15s
  • Jog OTS, Heels Up 15s
  • High Knee Jog 15s
  • High Knee Run 20s
  • Squat Burpee 30s
  • Bronco 25s
  • Bronco (x1), Block Jump (x1) 15s
  • Lateral Leap (L+R) 25s
  • Air Tap with Floor Tap 15s
  • Recovery

# 02. MONSTER SETS

  1. Bronco (x1), Block Jump (x1)
  2. Air Tap with Floor Tap
  3. Lateral Leap (L+R)
  4. High Knee Run

TIP

每个动作30秒, 5秒之后转换动作. 重复三轮.

# 03. 1 MINUTE CHALLENGE

  • Squat Burpee
  • Donkey Kick (x1)
  • Tuck Jump (x1)
  • Recovery

TIP

一分钟挑战: 三个动作组合, 持续一分钟.

# 04. DESCENDING SUPERSETS

  • Bear 180° Pushup (Spider Crawl)
  • Squat Jump, Side Step (L+R)
  • Recovery

TIP

递减组: 做三轮, 每个动作分别做 40s, 30s, 20s, 各个轮次间休息 20s.
